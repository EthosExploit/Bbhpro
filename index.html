Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our bug bounty hunting course.

Do I need prior hacking experience? +

No! This course is designed for complete beginners but also includes advanced content for those with some experience. We start with the fundamentals and gradually build up to professional-level techniques. The first few modules are specifically designed to bring everyone up to speed with the necessary basics.

What tools do I need? +

You'll need a computer (Windows, Mac, or Linux) with at least 4GB RAM. We'll guide you through installing free tools like Burp Suite Community Edition, OWASP ZAP, and others. No expensive software required! For the best experience, we recommend a system with 8GB RAM or more if you plan to run virtual machines for some of the labs.

How much time should I dedicate? +

We recommend 5-10 hours per week to complete the course in 2-3 months. However, you get lifetime access, so you can learn at your own pace. The more time you spend practicing, the faster you'll see results. Many successful students dedicate 15-20 hours per week and complete the course in about a month while building their bug hunting skills.

Will I really earn money from bug bounties? +

While we can't guarantee earnings (as results depend on your effort and skill development), many students have earned their first bounty within weeks of completing the course. We teach you exactly what companies pay for and how to find those vulnerabilities. Our top student has earned over $100,000 in bug bounties after completing the course.

Is this course legal? +

Absolutely! We only teach ethical hacking techniques for authorized testing. You'll learn how to participate in legal bug bounty programs and avoid any legal issues. Ethical hacking is a legitimate and growing career field with many opportunities for those with the right skills and ethical approach.
